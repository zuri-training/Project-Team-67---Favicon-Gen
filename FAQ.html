Get to know the questions users frequently asked from Favipro

What is Favicons?

A favicon is usually a tiny pixel icon that is displayed near every website you ever visit. Starting from the size of 16×16 pixels and going more prominent, it mainly represents your branding, and it is a huge helper when your visitor has multiple tabs open in their browsers.

How do I generate Favicons?

To generate a favicon on favipro, you simply have to create an account or sign in to an existing account if you have one. Once signed in, the generator options would be shown on your dashboard and you can select what format you would like to generate from.

What format can I get my favicons?

On favipro, you can get your favicons in PNG, SVG and ICO formats.

Is this a aid or free platform?

We are very generous so it is a free platform.

How many favicons can I get with my account?

You can generate unlimited favicons on favipro!

Can I re-download my favicon?

Yes you can, simply check your history page once signed in and it’s all there.

What type of image should I use for favicons?

You can use images in PNG or SVG format and with a maximum size of 5mb.

What are the avialable favicon sizes?

Favicons on favipro are available in 16x16px, 24x24px and 32x32px.
